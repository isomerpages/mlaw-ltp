The Ministry of Law ("MinLaw") launched the Technology and Innovation Roadmap ("TIR") in 2020, a 10-year plan that guides MinLaw's Legal Technology ("legaltech") efforts.

The Legal Technology Platform Initiative ("LTPI") is a key initiative under the TIR to enhance the technology adoption rate among small and medium Singapore law practices ("SLPs")
